Snake, Water, and Gun Game 🐍💧🔫
This is a Python implementation of the classic Snake, Water, and Gun game, a variation of Rock, Paper, Scissors. The player competes against the computer, and the game randomly decides the winner based on the following rules:

Snake drinks Water but is shot by Gun.
Water douses Gun but is swallowed by Snake.
Gun shoots Snake but gets doused by Water.
Features:
Randomized outcomes: The computer randomly selects its choice for each round.
User Input: Players can choose between Snake, Water, or Gun.
Result Display: Shows the winner after each round and tracks the overall score.
Fun Interaction: A fun way to engage with simple conditional logic and game loops!
